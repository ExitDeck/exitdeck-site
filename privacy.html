Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 12, 2025

ExitDeck (“the App”) is designed to keep your data private. We do not collect, store, or sell personal data.

Data We Store

  • On-device portfolio data. Your holdings and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync (optional). If you enable iCloud, your data is synced via Apple’s iCloud using your Apple ID. ExitDeck does not have access to your iCloud data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration with ExitDeck.
  • No analytics or tracking across apps or websites.
  • No third‑party advertising SDKs.

Crash Reporting (Optional)

If you enable Apple’s “Share with App Developers” or use Crashlytics, anonymized crash data may be sent. This helps us fix bugs. It contains no portfolio contents.

Security

Your iCloud data is protected by Apple. We recommend securing your device with Face ID/Touch ID and a passcode.
