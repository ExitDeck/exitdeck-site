Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 12, 2025

ExitDeck (“the App”) is designed to keep your data private. We do not sell your personal data or use third-party advertising trackers.

Data We Store

  • On-device portfolio & journal data. Your holdings, targets, notes, and journal entries are stored locally on your device only. They are not sent to our servers.
  • Alert preferences. If you turn on price/target alerts, we may store your alert thresholds and a push notification token so we can send alerts to your device. We do not access your exchange accounts or private keys.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No exchange API keys or wallet private keys.
  • No account registration with ExitDeck is currently required.
  • No cross-app tracking or third-party advertising SDKs.

Crash Reporting (Optional)

If you enable Apple’s “Share with App Developers” setting, anonymized crash data may be sent to us through Apple. This information helps us identify bugs and improve stability. It does not include your portfolio contents, exchange credentials, or personal financial data.

Purchases & Subscriptions

All payments and subscriptions are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. ExitDeck does not receive or store your full payment card or bank details. We may receive anonymized information from Apple about your active subscription status (for example, whether your subscription is active or expired) so that the app can unlock the correct features.

Security

Your data is protected by the security of your device. We recommend securing your device with Face ID / Touch ID and a strong passcode, keeping your operating system up to date, and using reputable exchanges or hardware wallets for actual crypto custody.

Your Choices

  • You can turn notifications on or off in iOS Settings > Notifications.
  • You may delete the app at any time to remove locally stored data from your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: support@exitdeck.app
